• There are two players.
  • Each player has a round score and a global score.
  • At the beginning in a round, the players current score will be 0 and the player can roll the dice as many time as wish. The round score will be added in current box.
  • During his turn, the player can add his score to the global score by clicking "Hold" button. This will be the next player's turn.
  • If the player roll the number 1, he will lost his round score and it will be the next player's turn.
  • The one who reach 100 Global Score will be the Winner!
